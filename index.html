Joe Cooper's Top Tens of the Tens!

Hey thanks for visiting!

This is just a simple project displaying my top ten albums over a number of years. I love music and obsessively rank my favorite albums in my head each year. I thought it might be a fun personal project to develop a fancy little page that shows off my AJAX/JSON talents.

You can begin your journey by clicking on a year up above. From there, you can either click through or use the arrow keys on your keyboard to navigate around the page.
